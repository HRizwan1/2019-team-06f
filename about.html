What is TruHawk?

TruHawk is a photosharing platform for IIT students and faculty to share their hawk life. This website provides a space for students and staff to get engaged with the IIT community. It's like our private repository. No one knows our secrets! Shhh!

Truhawk is inspired by Flickr and Instagram.

What Is Our Mission?

Our goal as up and coming IT professionals is to provide Illinois Tech with a storage solution for IIT photos while at the same time building our educational skills as students with the university.

How to Get Started!

  1. Join Us!

    2. Sign Up! All you need is a IIT email account.

  2. Start Browsing

    3. Once you set up your account, you are free to search photos, find your friends, comment and start engaging!

  3. Your Turn!

    4. Start uploading your own photos to share your interesting finds of the day. We would love to see your face!
